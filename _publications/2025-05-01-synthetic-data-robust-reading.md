**Abstract:** We design a leakage-free document OCR data synthesis pipeline that expands Wikipedia seeds via LLM and fuses LaTeX tables to generate Markdown documents. After rendering through TeX Live + PyMuPDF, realistic degradations (illumination, creases, ink blots) are applied via Augraphy. We introduce an LLM error-correction paradigm for post-hoc rectification of OCR outputs, addressing three critical challenges: data leakage in public corpora, absence of realistic noise in synthetic data, and elimination of long-tail OCR errors.

**Keywords:** OCR, Synthetic Data, Document Understanding, LLM Correction, LaTeX Rendering
